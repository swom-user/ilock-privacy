iLock Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 17, 2025

1. Information Collection and Use

iLock does not collect any personal information from users.

  • We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as names, emails, or phone numbers
  • We do not collect location information
  • We do not access contacts, photos, files, or other personal data

2. Local Data Storage

iLock stores the following information locally on your device only and does not transmit it externally:

  • Onboarding completion status (app settings)
  • Screen lock state (temporary state for app functionality)

This data is deleted when you uninstall the app.

3. Permissions Used

iLock uses the following permission:

  • "Display over other apps" permission: Required to display the floating button and screen lock overlay

This permission is used solely for the app's core functionality (screen locking) and is not used for any other purposes.

4. Information Sharing with Third Parties

Since iLock does not collect user information, there is no information shared with third parties.

  • No advertising networks are used
  • No analytics tools are used
  • No communication with external servers

5. Data Security

iLock does not collect personal information, so there is no data stored on or transmitted to servers. All app settings are securely stored only on your device.

6. Children's Privacy

iLock does not collect personal information from users of any age, including children. The app is designed to be safe for children to use.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated due to legal or service changes. Changes will be posted on this page and the "Last Updated" date will be updated.
